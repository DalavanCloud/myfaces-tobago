<tc:toolBar/> and <tc:toolBarCommand/> are deprecated. To replace a toolbar in the header of a component use or instead. To replace a plain toolbar, use .

The first box show the deprecated version of a toolbar. The second and the third one are solutions with #{overviewBundle.buttons} and #{overviewBundle.commands}.

Instead of <f:facet name="toolBar"/> use <f:facet name="bar"/>.

Instead of <tc:toolBar/> use <tc:buttons/> or <tc:commands/>.

Instead of <tc:toolBarCommand/> use <tc:button/> or <tc:link/>.

For a plain toolbar use the <tc:bar/> tag.

<tc:toolBar>
  <tc:toolBarCommand label="Text Button"/>
  <tc:toolBarCommand label="Image Button" image="fa-cube"/>
  <tc:in placeholder="Search"/>
</tc:toolBar>
 
<tc:bar>
  <tc:buttons>
    <tc:button label="Text Button"/>
    <tc:button label="Image Button" image="fa-cube"/>
  </tc:buttons>
  <tc:flowLayout textAlign="right">
    <tc:button label="Dropdown" omit="true"> ... </tc:button>
    <tc:in placeholder="Search"/>
  </tc:flowLayout>
</tc:bar>
